# 📊 CRM Sales Pipeline Analysis (SQL Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a B2B sales pipeline dataset of a fictitious computer hardware company. The goal is to evaluate sales performance, identify underperforming agents, analyze trends over time, and determine product effectiveness using SQL.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Evaluate performance of sales teams
* Identify underperforming sales agents
* Analyze quarter-over-quarter revenue trends
* Determine product-wise win rates

---

## 🗂️ Dataset Description

The dataset includes the following tables:

* **sales_pipeline** → Contains deal-level data such as sales agent, product, deal stage, revenue, and close date
* **accounts** → Customer account information
* **products** → Product details
* **data_dictionary** → Metadata about dataset

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

* SQL (MySQL)
* Data Aggregation (`SUM`, `COUNT`, `AVG`)
* Conditional Logic (`CASE WHEN`)
* Window Functions (`LAG`)
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Business Analysis

---

## 📊 Key Analysis & Insights

### 1️⃣ Sales Team Performance

* Compared total deals, revenue, and win rate across sales agents
* Identified top-performing and low-performing agents

### 2️⃣ Underperforming Agents

* Detected agents with low revenue and win rates
* Highlighted potential need for training or strategy improvement

### 3️⃣ Quarter-over-Quarter Trends

* Analyzed revenue and deal count over time
* Identified growth patterns and seasonal trends
* Used window functions to calculate revenue growth

### 4️⃣ Product Performance

* Compared win rates across products
* Identified high-performing and low-performing products

---

## 📈 Key Metrics Used

* Total Deals
* Total Revenue
* Win Rate (%)
* Quarterly Growth

---

## 💡 Business Impact

* Helps management identify strong and weak sales performers
* Supports strategic decision-making for product focus
* Enables tracking of sales growth trends over time
